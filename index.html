About Us

Mydiabolo team was found in the year of 2004 by Mr. Chan Chee Ee, it is a professional performance team with more than 10 years of performing experiences. All the performances are act in concert with the trained Diabolo skill and the musical melody. Our Mydiabolo Team has been participating and won in many kind of domestic and overseas competitions in these few years including competition in Japan, Taiwan, Singapore and so on. Other than that, Mydiabolo Team has been traveling around Malaysia to undertake various types of performances and also be recognised by the audience.
